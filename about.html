About me

Hello! My name is Nylce Joy M. Grejarte. I believe in the importance of continuously developing skills and embracing lifelong learning. To achieve your goals, you need to work hard, stay passionate, and never quit. My dream is to become a professional software engineer. After graduating with a degree in IT, I worked in various banks as a money sorter. Although this role provided valuable experience, I soon realized it was not aligned with my true passion for technology and software development. This realization prompted me to pursue my dream career in software engineering. In my journey to achieve this dream, I have dedicated myself to expanding my knowledge and expertise in web and graphic design. This portfolio showcases my ability to create visually compelling and user-friendly websites, where I seamlessly blend creativity with functionality. Outside of my professional aspirations, I am a coffee enthusiast who enjoys exploring new coffee shops and brewing techniques. Coffee fuels my creativity and productivity, making it an essential part of my daily routine. Welcome to my world!
